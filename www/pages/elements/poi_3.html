    The Neptune Fountain POI

    The Neptune Fountain is a monumental statuesque piece depicting Greek god Neptune in a shell-chariot, being drawn by four sea-horses and heralded by conch-shell blowing merman.
    Neptune sits proudly, clothed in a mantle while clasping a Trident as a symbolic representation of his dominion.
    The fountain in Cheltenham is worth a visit all year round; whether water flows from its faucets or not
